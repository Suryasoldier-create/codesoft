Mahendra Singh Dhoni, popularly known as **Captain Cool**, is one of India's greatest cricketers. His leadership, calmness, and match-finishing abilities have made him an icon.

Under his captaincy, India won the **2007 T20 World Cup, 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and 2013 Champions Trophy**. He is known for his incredible wicket-keeping skills and innovative captaincy.

Dhoni's ability to stay calm under pressure and his inspiring leadership make him one of the most **admired** sports personalities in the world.
